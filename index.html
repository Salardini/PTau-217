Welcome to the Alzheimer's Disease Risk Calculator

This evidence-based tool provides personalized Alzheimer's disease risk estimates using plasma pTau 217/Î²-Amyloid 1-42 ratio biomarker testing combined with demographic and genetic factors. Developed from FDA-validated clinical data (K242706) encompassing 499 patients across the cognitive spectrum, the calculator integrates validated likelihood ratios with established epidemiological data to support clinical decision-making in specialized care settings. The risk assessment considers age, cognitive status, APOE genotype, and biomarker results to provide both annual and 10-year risk estimates, enabling healthcare providers to make informed decisions about diagnostic workup, monitoring strategies, and intervention planning.
